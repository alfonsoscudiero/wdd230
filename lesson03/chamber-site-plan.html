Overview

Site Name

Utah County Chamber of Commerce

This name reflects the regional focus on Utah County and emphasizes the chamber's role in connecting businesses and community.

Purpose

  • Promote Business Growth: The site will serve as a hub for promoting local businesses, offering networking opportunities, and spotlighting business activities, news, and events.
  • Community Engagement: It will be a resource for visitors and locals alike, providing information on area attractions, events, and community services. It will also include tools to support new business ventures and foster economic development.

Personas

  1. Local Business Owner (Jessica)
    Jessica owns a small bakery and is looking for opportunities to network, promote her business, and stay updated on local events that attract customers to the area.
  2. New Resident (David)
    David recently moved to Utah County. He is interested in exploring the community and learning about local services, businesses, and events that can help him settle in.
  3. Visitor/Traveler (Liz)
    Liz is visiting Utah County for a conference. She wants to discover attractions, local dining options, and events during her stay.

Scenarios

Question 1: What benefits can my business get from joining the chamber, and how do I apply for membership?

Question 2: What are some local events happening this weekend, and where can I explore parks and attractions?

Question 3: Which restaurants or local businesses should I check out while I am in town, and are there any cultural events or tours I can attend?
